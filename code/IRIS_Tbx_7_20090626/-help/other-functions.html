select

Retrieve submatrices from ACF, ACFD, FFRF, IFRF, or XSF matrices for a selection of variables.

Syntax

[X,selection] = select(X,list,selection...)
[d,selection] = select(d,list,selection...)

Output arguments

X[numeric]Submatrix with rows and columns corresponding to the selection of variables.
d[struct]Database with time series columns corresponding to the selection of comments.
selection[cell | cellstr]List of actually retrieved variables.

Input arguments

X[model]Matrix obtained as a result of acf, acfd, ffrf, fevd, ifrf, or xsf.
d[struct]Database with multivariate time series obtained as a result of e.g. simulate with 'contributions' set to true, or srf.
list[cellstr]List of variables in row and columns of the original matrix X.
selection[cell | cellstr]List of requested variables.

Description

Example

In the following example, we assume that m is a solved model object with one parameterisation, and x, y, z are three of its variables (whether measurement or transition). C1 will be a 3 × 3 × 2 matrix with the contemporaneous and first-order covariances for the three variables only. 

[C,R,list] = acf(m,'order',1);
C1 = select(C,list,{'x','y','z'});
